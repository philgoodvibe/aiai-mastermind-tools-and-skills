# brain/ - What MAYA Knows About Your Business

This folder is MAYA's memory. Everything MAYA writes and designs is grounded in what lives here. Generic input produces generic content, so the more honest and specific this folder is, the less your posts sound like AI.

## Put your agency context here

Drop your **My_Agency_Context.md** into this folder. You built this document in the Agency Setup Assistant walkthrough (a prerequisite for this course). MAYA reads it before every post for:

- WHO I SERVE (your ideal client personas)
- MY COMMUNITY (local context, events, languages)
- MY BRAND (exact color hex codes, voice descriptors, visual style)
- MY STORY (background, differentiators)
- MY RULES (your compliance baseline)
- Agency name and contact info (phone, email, website)

If any of those sections are thin, MAYA will ask. Answer once, and it stays answered.

## What else belongs in here

- `compliance/` - the carrier-safe language rules. Starts as a template. You and MAYA fill it in for your carrier and state, and it grows every time a draft gets corrected.
- `voice/` - your voice profile (required) and your story bank (optional). This is what separates your feed from every other agent's feed. The voice profile is the required core; set it up first. The story bank is optional but highly recommended and you can add it later. See voice/README.md for both interview prompts.

## Optional additions that make MAYA sharper

- Past posts that performed well (paste them in a file called `what-worked.md`)
- Posts that flopped or felt off (`what-didnt-work.md`)
- Notes on local events, seasons, and community rhythms MAYA should plan around

One rule for this whole folder: facts and stories only, never secrets. No API keys, no passwords, no client personal data. Keys live in the keychain, and client stories get names removed before they enter the story bank.
