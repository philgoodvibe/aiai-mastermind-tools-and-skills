# voice/ - Your Voice and Your Stories

This folder is why your content will not sound like everyone else's AI content. Two files live here, and MAYA maintains both.

## 1. voice-profile.md (you or MAYA creates this)

A description of how you actually talk: signature phrases, sentence patterns, how you open, how you land a point, the words you would never use. MAYA checks every caption against it.

Where it comes from, best to worst:

1. **Transcripts of you talking.** If you have meeting recordings, voicemails, voice memos, or videos, give MAYA the transcripts and say: "Build my voice profile from how I actually speak in these transcripts."
2. **Your My Voice document** from the AIAI social media workshop, if you completed it. Drop it in this folder and tell MAYA to adapt it.
3. **An interview.** No recordings? Tell MAYA: "Interview me for 10 minutes about my business, then build my voice profile from my answers."

Note for the profile: your speaking voice may be more intense or more rambling than what works on social. MAYA should keep the flavor and tighten the form, and adjust as your feedback comes in.

## 2. story-bank.md (built by the interview below)

A bank of 20 to 30 real stories from your work and life, names removed. They do not have to be insurance stories. They have to show challenge, success, and progress: how you take care of business and people. This is what makes posts personal instead of generic.

### The MAYA interview prompt

Paste this into Claude Code when you have 30 to 45 minutes:

```
Interview me to build my story bank. Ask me one question at a time and dig
for the specifics: what happened, who was involved (no real names in the
final version), what went wrong, what it cost, what changed, and what I
learned. I want 20 to 30 stories total. Cover these areas:

- Why I got into this industry, and what almost made me quit
- The client situations I am proudest of
- Claims or losses that taught me something
- Times I told a client a hard truth
- My community: who I serve, in their words
- Mistakes I made early and what I do differently now
- Family and personal moments that shaped how I do business
- Wins that had nothing to do with insurance

After the interview, write each story into brain/voice/story-bank.md with:
a title, the story in 3 to 6 sentences in my voice, names and private
details removed, and a tag for which content pillar it serves
(Relate, Reveal, Guide, or Invite).
```

You can do this in more than one sitting. Tell MAYA "continue my story bank interview" and it picks up where you left off.

## How MAYA uses this folder

- Every caption gets checked against voice-profile.md before it is called done.
- Story-driven posts pull from story-bank.md instead of inventing anecdotes. Invented client stories are a compliance failure.
- When you give voice feedback on a draft ("I would never say that," "too stiff," "more like how I talk to clients"), MAYA updates voice-profile.md the same day.
