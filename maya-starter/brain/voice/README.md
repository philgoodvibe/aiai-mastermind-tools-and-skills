# voice/ - Your Voice and Your Stories

This folder is why your content will not sound like everyone else's AI content. Two things live here. One is required, one is optional.

- **voice-profile.md** is the required core. It is how you talk, and MAYA gates every caption against it. Without it, your feed sounds like generic AI.
- **story-bank.md** is optional but highly recommended. It is a bank of your real stories, and it is what lets MAYA write personal-story posts. You can add it later.

Two ready-to-paste prompts and one template do the work for you:

- `voice-interview-prompt.md` - the prompt that makes MAYA interview you and write your voice profile.
- `voice-profile-template.md` - the structure MAYA produces, so you know what a finished voice profile contains.
- `story-bank-interview-prompt.md` - the prompt that makes MAYA interview you for your story bank, when you are ready.

## 1. voice-profile.md (required core)

A description of how you actually talk: your speaking-style summary, pace and rhythm, sentence patterns, vocabulary, signature phrases, explanation style, emotional tone, audience relationship, personality markers, a Tuning Fork Sentence, and a Voice Matching Checklist. MAYA checks every future caption against this file. See `voice-profile-template.md` for the full structure.

Set this up first. It is the difference between sounding like you and sounding like generic AI, so it is required training, not extra credit.

How to build it, best to worst:

1. **The voice interview.** Open `voice-interview-prompt.md`, paste it into the Claude Code panel, and answer MAYA's 10 questions one at a time. Answer by voice for best fidelity (record and paste, or dictate), or type loose and unedited. MAYA preserves your real phrasing, does not polish it, and does not invent verbal tics it never heard. When the interview is done, MAYA writes `voice-profile.md` for you.
2. **Transcripts of you talking.** If you have meeting recordings, voicemails, voice memos, or videos, give MAYA the transcripts and say: "Build my voice profile from how I actually speak in these transcripts," following the template structure.
3. **Your My Voice document** from the AIAI social media workshop, if you completed it. Drop it in this folder and tell MAYA to adapt it to the template structure.

Note for the profile: your speaking voice may be more intense or more rambling than what works on social. MAYA should keep the flavor and tighten the form, and adjust as your feedback comes in.

## 2. story-bank.md (optional, highly recommended)

A bank of 20 to 30 real stories from your work and life, names removed, each tagged to a content pillar (Relate, Reveal, Guide, or Invite). They do not have to be insurance stories. They have to show challenge, success, and progress: how you take care of business and people. This is what makes posts personal instead of generic.

Optional but highly recommended. Set up the system first and add stories later. If you do not have it, do not worry. Without it, MAYA still works from your voice profile and your agency context; it simply will not write personal-story posts until you add it. You can come back and add it any time.

When you are ready, open `story-bank-interview-prompt.md`, paste it into the Claude Code panel, and answer the questions. Plan for 30 to 45 minutes, or split it across sittings. Tell MAYA "continue my story bank interview" and it picks up where you left off.

## How MAYA uses this folder

- Every caption gets gated against the Voice Matching Checklist in voice-profile.md before it is called done.
- Story-driven posts pull from story-bank.md instead of inventing anecdotes. If story-bank.md does not exist, MAYA skips personal-story posts rather than inventing them. Invented client stories are a compliance failure.
- When you give voice feedback on a draft ("I would never say that," "too stiff," "more like how I talk to clients"), MAYA updates voice-profile.md the same day.
