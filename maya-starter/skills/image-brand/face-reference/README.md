# face-reference/ - The Owner's Reference Photos

Drop 5 to 10 clear photos of yourself (the owner) into this folder. These are the only face images MAYA and Codex are allowed to use when generating visuals. Nobody else's photo goes in here, ever.

## What makes a good reference photo

- Your face clearly visible and in focus, taking up a good portion of the frame
- Well lit, no heavy shadows across the face, no sunglasses
- A mix of angles: straight on, slight left, slight right
- A mix of expressions: warm smile, neutral, mid-laugh
- Recent, within the last year or two, looking how you look now
- Plain or uncluttered backgrounds work best

## What to skip

- Group photos (crop yourself out first if it is the only good shot)
- Hats, sunglasses, or anything hiding the face
- Heavy filters or beauty-mode shots; they teach the model a face that is not yours
- Photos of anyone else, including family and staff. The rule is absolute: only the owner appears in generated images.

## Naming

Simple names are fine: `front-smile.jpg`, `left-neutral.jpg`, `laughing.jpg`. MAYA picks the photos that match the pose each image needs.

Note: clothing in these photos does not constrain the output. MAYA tells Codex to match your face and likeness, not what you are wearing.
